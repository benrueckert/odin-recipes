Best Scrambled Eggs

Description

For when you want a classic plate of cheesy comfort food, these Best Scrambled Eggs are sure to satisfy. They're quick enough for weekday breakfasts too, taking just eight minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 thin slices cooked ham, diced (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream
  • Â½ teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk eggs in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in ham, Cheddar cheese, and heavy cream.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
